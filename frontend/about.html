Hotel Policies

General Policies

Check-In: From 3 PM
Check-Out: Until 11 AM
Payment: Payment is required prior to check-in, and can be made using either cash or card.
Early Check-In / Late Check-Out: Subject to availability and additional charges.
Right to Refuse Service: We Reserve the Right to Refuse Service to Anyone

Reservation Policies

Reservations: Please reserve your room online or call us.
Check-Reservations: You can manage your reservation online with your last name and Booking ID. If you don't know your Booking ID, please call us, and one of our staff members will assist you.
Deposit: A cash deposit of $100 is required at the time of check-in.
Cancellation: You can cancel your reservation at any time online or by phone. No-show within one day of your check-in duration will be dropped from reservation.

Room Policies

Occupancy: Maximum of 2 guests for single bed room and 4 for double bed room.
Smoking: We have smoking and non smoking options for Rooms.
Pets: No pets allowed unles service animal.
Service animal:We welcome service animals at our hotel and are committed to providing a comfortable and accessible stay for all our guests. While we do not charge a pet fee for service animals, a cleaning fee will be applied if additional cleaning is required due to the presence of the service animal.

General Amenities and Services

Wi-Fi: Complimentary throughout the hotel.
Parking: We offer valet parking services to enhance your stay with us. Our valet parking is available 24/7 with no additional fees.
Housekeeping: Daily housekeeping service provided.

Safety and Security

Fire Safety: Please familiarize yourself with fire exits and evacuation procedures whihc are located on your floor or behind your room door.
Emergency Contacts: Dial 0 for in-house emergencies, 911 for local emergencies.

Guest Responsibilities

Damage Policy: Guests are liable for any damages to hotel property.
Behavior: We expect all guests to behave respectfully. Illegal activities are prohibited.

Special Policies

Accessibility: Contact us for special assistance or accommodations.

Local Attractions

Universal Studios Hollywood: Explore the movie-themed amusement park with thrilling rides and behind-the-scenes tours in Los Angeles.
